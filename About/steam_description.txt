[h1]RimSynapse - Regions and Territories[/h1]
[b]Overhauls the world map generation with dynamic geographic province generation, world map overlays, and territory borders.[/b]

Regions and Territories divides the globe into dynamic geographic provinces with defined borders, population densities, custom road linkages, and procedurally placed ruins.

---

[h2]Key Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Geographic Domain Mapping:[/b] Splits the world map into dynamic provinces with clear geographic boundaries.
[*] [b]Faction Territory Overlays:[/b] Displays visual, expandable territory control maps using the Map Mode Framework.
[*] [b]Procedural Trade Infrastructure:[/b] Dynamically links generated settlements with trade roads and places historical ruins on the world map.
[/list]

---

[h2]Changelog (v0.5.0)[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Odyssey DLC Compatibility:[/b] Solved the duplicate faction placement issue by short-circuiting non-surface layers. Space and Orbit factions (like the Traders Guild) now spawn correctly in space, while terrestrial factions are cleanly placed into geographic domains.
[*] [b]Faction Layer Filtering:[/b] Integrates the game's internal layer constraints (using reflected FactionGenerator.CanExistOnLayer), preventing orbital factions from generating on land.
[/list]
